About Me

My name is Anastasia, and I am an English language teacher with experience working in various schools, universities, online schools, and volunteering in Germany, the Netherlands, and India. I have always wanted to be a teacher, I love my job, and I enjoy developing in it.

In addition to my diploma from the pedagogical university, my education includes a diploma from Erasmus University in Rotterdam on the basics of business, as well as certificates from Google in IT and Digital Marketing, confirming my language skills. My teaching programs align with the latest trends and allow for learning English through children's favorite games. Isn't it wonderful when learning takes the form of a game? It is not only engaging but also effective for grasping the material. I have previously taught in schools, but I realized that some school methods do not always meet the needs of modern children. Most school directors seem to be adults who are stuck in the past and reluctant to keep up with the times and follow modern trends. I am confident that English lessons can be engaging and more effective by incorporating game elements that children love.

I am ready to offer your child exciting lessons, full of games and educational moments. Let's create an atmosphere where your little one will joyfully learn English!

What do you need to know

My philosophy of teaching is based on creative methodologies that make the process of learning English interesting and inspiring

Each of my lessons is structured to incorporate communicative skills, helping children confidently express their thoughts and feelings in English, while supporting their interest and motivation. I actively integrate modern technologies into the teaching process. The use of interactive online resources, artificial intelligence, and educational applications makes lessons more engaging and accessible. One of the key principles of my work is adaptability and individualization for each student. I strive to create a unique approach for every child, considering their needs and learning pace.

These methods are not just theory – I have seen their effectiveness in practice. I am confident that innovative approaches to teaching allow my students not only to master English but also to develop a love for the learning process!
